Joe Henry Engle served as a pilot in the USAF, a test pilot of the X-15 and a Nasa Astronaut. (source: wikipedia)

  • Born August 26, 1932 in Abilene, Kansas
  • Bachelor of Science degree in Areospace Engineering from the University in Kansas in 1955
  • Entered flying school in 1957 and selected by NASA in 1966
  • Served as a test pilot at Edwards Air Force Base. He flew the X-15.
  • Served as Space Shuttle commander for two missions for NASA
  • Retired from the USAF and NASA in 1986
  • Date of Birth: 8/26/1932
